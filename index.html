Bio

I am a last-year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Universite Laval (UL) working under supervision of Christian Gagne and Denis Laurendeau. My current research revolves around dependeble and secure learning models to adversarial examples and Out-of-Distribution samples. Before joining UL, I was a researcher master student at Digitial Media Lab of Sharif University of Technology(SUT), working on 3D human pose estimation under supervision H. Reza. Rabiee. I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from SUT. As my BSc project, I studied and investigated the community detection algorithms for detecting communities of Iranian Mathematicians around the globe.
